Looking at the Right Talent

We always look forward to hiring energetic talents over the senior experienced ones immediately. We want the right set of people who align with our vision in helping us serve customers better. We have seen that having the multi-tasking edge allows us to work across different teams easily, this has given us the required productive output to deliver excellence.

Collaborative Work Atmosphere

Building innovation inside the company requires a lot of knowledge sharing and exchange of ideas. We ensure and encourage active collaboration and engagement with all our stakeholders. Keeping an open mind has helped us gained more insights as to how we can engage with the end users effectively and efficiently.

Ownership as against Reporting

Reporting system have proven to be barriers in fostering talents. We found that having a horizontal transparent reporting system improved communication among all the team members, which further kindled knowledge sharing, increased productivity and achieved common goals. We went a little further and made the junior team members take ownership of the projects, this showed incredible increase in efficiency and effective delivery of tight time bound projects.

Adopting to Design Thinking

These are one of those ‘wow’ moments we have while building products. Having a multidisciplinary team and empathy for customers starts at ideation itself. Design thinking methodology has definitely helped us in solving problems at every stage and achieving the milestones faster through active interaction and feedback with all stakeholders

Building a Strong Engineering Practise

Having different engineering minds working on a single project exhibits a strong process and a strong communication internals. We have been practicing “Extreme Programming Practices” and have added own flavors along with Agile methods to make it more adaptable into our work environment.

Skill Adoption based on Hybrid Roles

Being adaptable with the situation is what made us grow over the period of years. We allowed talents to work on cross platforms and upskill themselves. Having made to wear multiple hats, the stats showed that individuals started empathizing for the customers better and performed harder to achieve the business goals.

Not the Idea! But the Implementation

For small organizations, implementation has always been a challenging task. Realizing this we concentrated on the implementation process keeping the base idea intact. This process allowed us to build the product with greater understanding of the end user needs cutting down the assumptions.
